## üé® Design Consistency

### Across All Screens
1. **Navy headers** with gold accents
2. **Ivory/Cream backgrounds** for content
3. **Serif typography** for body text
4. **Display font** for headings with extreme tracking
5. **Corner ornaments** on elevated cards
6. **Geometric dividers** between major sections
7. **Gold gradients** for borders and accents (never solid gold blocks)
8. **Symmetrical layouts** with center alignment
9. **Generous spacing** and whitespace
10. **Stepped decorations** and geometric patterns

### Interactive States
- **Hover**: Borders change from navy/20% to gold/60%
- **Focus**: Gold borders become solid
- **Active**: Gold color for navigation items
- **Disabled**: Reduced opacity

### Animations
- **Duration**: 200ms for colors, 300ms for transforms
- **Easing**: ease-in-out for smooth transitions
- **Shimmer**: 700ms gold gradient sweep on button hover
- **Pulse**: Loading dots with staggered delays

**Every screen embodies the Art Deco ethos: luxurious, geometric, symmetrical, and editorial. The app feels like browsing a premium 1930s magazine on a modern device.**
